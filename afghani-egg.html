The Afghani Egg Recipe


Description

afghans cook the egg differently.
and also they always eats it with bread
the cooking process is simple.
and it takes about 10-15 minutes


Ingredients

it does not need so much things
that is just:

  • egg
  • salt
  • pepper
  • onion
  • tomato

How To Cook

  1. first cut onion and tomatoes in to small pieces.

  2. pour some oil in to frying pan.
    put the pan on a slow fire.

  3. let it be warm.then drop the cuted
    onions in to pan let it be fried.

  4. then add cuted tomatoes on it.
    add so little amount of warm water on it
    to make that a little liquid.

  5. mix it for 2 minutes.then drop eggs.

  6. mix them so slowly untill the egg's
    liquid parts be a little solid.

  7. drop the salt and the pepper while mixing.

  8. finally you have an afghani egg.
    eat that with bread and enjoy it's flavor .
    at the end if you like vegetables
    you can add some on it.
