The Boiled Potato Recipe


Description:

the boiled potato food is one
of the most regular foods of
afghans and iranians.
this food is so useful.
because it does not containes.
any oil when cooking.
the cooking process is so simple.
every one can cook this food.


Ingredients

that is not so much it is:

  • some potatoes
  • water
  • salt
  • pepper


How To Cook

  1. that is easy.
    first wash cleanly the potatoes.

  2. pour some water inside steam boiler.
    now drop potatoes to steam boiler.

  3. add the salt and pepper now.
    and close the steam boiler's head.

  4. boil it for 30 minutes.

  5. yes that is cooked and you can
    enjoy this healty food.
