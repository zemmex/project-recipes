The Afghani Kebbab


Ingredients

  • beef
  • salt
  • pepper


Descriptions

the afghani kebbab is so simple.
it is so delicious.
and it is generally fried
with broiler and coal in a
natural way.


How To Cook

  1. cut the beff in to small pieces about 3cm3.

  2. now warm the broiler with hot coals

  3. push skewer inside cuted beefs.
    put the skewer on the broiler.

  4. fresh the coals with shaking the fan.
    do it fastly.while shaking the fan
    turn around the skewers.do it
    while the beef became soft and cooked

  5. after the beff cooked.pour the
    salt and pepper on it.get the bread
    and enjoy the afghani kebbab.
